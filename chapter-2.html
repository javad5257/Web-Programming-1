Money Heist

Money Heist (La Casa de Papel) is a Spanish heist crime drama television series created by Álex Pina. It follows a group of robbers who execute meticulously planned heists on the Royal Mint of Spain and the Bank of Spain under the guidance of their mysterious leader, The Professor.

Key Information

Genre: Crime, Thriller, Drama

Created by: Álex Pina

Release Date: May 2, 2017

Cast & Creative Team

  • Álvaro Morte as The Professor
  • Úrsula Corberó as Tokyo
  • Pedro Alonso as Berlin
  • Itziar Ituño as Raquel Murillo
  • Miguel Herrán as Rio

Main Characters

  • The Professor: The mastermind behind the heists.
  • Tokyo: The rebellious and impulsive narrator.
  • Berlin: The arrogant but charismatic second-in-command.
  • Raquel Murillo: A police negotiator who becomes involved with the heist.

Reviews

Money Heist has received critical acclaim for its gripping plot, well-developed characters, and thrilling twists, making it one of Netflix’s most popular international series.
